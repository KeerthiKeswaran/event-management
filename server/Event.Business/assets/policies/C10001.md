# Cancellation and Booking Policy

**Last Updated:** June 26, 2026  
**Version:** v1.0  
**Policy ID:** C10001

This Cancellation and Booking Policy outlines the rules and procedures regarding ticket bookings, payments, and cancellations for all events hosted on our Platform.

---

## 1. Ticket Bookings and Reservations
1.1. When reserving tickets, your seats are temporarily locked for a period of ten (10) minutes to complete the checkout process. If payment is not completed within this timeframe, the tickets will be released back to the general pool.
1.2. Users may book a maximum of 10 tickets per transaction as per platform policy limits to prevent scalping.

---

## 2. Dynamic Refund Calculation Schedule
2.1. If you cancel your confirmed ticket booking, refund amounts are calculated dynamically based on when the cancellation is requested:
   - **Cancellation > 48 Hours prior to Event Start:** 90% refund of the original ticket purchase price.
   - **Cancellation between 12 and 48 Hours prior to Event Start:** 50% refund of the original ticket purchase price.
   - **Cancellation < 12 Hours prior to Event Start:** No refund will be processed.
2.2. If the Event Organizer cancels the event, all ticket holders will automatically receive a 100% refund of their ticket purchase price.

---

## 3. Refund Processing and Stripe Gateway
3.1. Refund processing is integrated directly with Stripe. Approved refunds will be credited back to your original payment method.
3.2. Please allow up to five (5) to ten (10) business days for Stripe refunds to reflect in your banking statement depending on your financial institution.
