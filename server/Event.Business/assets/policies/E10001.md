# Event Organizer Creation Agreement

**Last Updated:** June 14, 2026  
**Version:** v1.0  
**Policy ID:** E100001

This Event Organizer Creation Agreement ("Agreement") governs the terms under which organizers may create, publish, and stage events on the Platform. By checking the policy acceptance box, you agree to be legally bound by this document.

---

## 1. Eligibility & Account Status
1.1. Only registered users in good standing may create events.
1.2. Organizers whose accounts are marked with a status of `Restricted` or `Deactivated` are strictly prohibited from creating new events.
1.3. The Platform reserves the right to suspend or deactivate accounts found to be in violation of these terms.

---

## 2. Event Types & Venue Allocation
2.1. Organizers can create events classified as **Virtual**, **Physical**, or **Hybrid**.
2.2. For Physical or Hybrid events, a valid and available Platform venue must be booked.
2.3. Venue occupancy checks are performed automatically. The Platform will reject scheduling if a venue is occupied during the requested timeslot (including setup and teardown periods).

---

## 3. Financial Terms, Fees & Commissions
3.1. **Upfront Activation Fee:** All events require payment of an upfront activation fee to cover platform hosting, security, and staffing logistics:
   - **Virtual Events:** Free / No Upfront Fee.
   - **Physical & Hybrid Events:** $100.00 USD flat activation fee.
3.2. **Ticket Commissions:** The Platform takes a service fee on all sold tickets:
   - A commission of **5.00%** on the ticket price.
   - A fixed ticketing fee of **$0.99 USD** per ticket.
3.3. **Organizer Payouts:** Net ticketing proceeds (gross sales minus ticketing commissions) are transferred to the organizer's payout bank account within five (5) business days after the successful completion of the event.

---

## 4. Staff Allocation Requirements
4.1. Physical and Hybrid events may opt to request Platform support staff.
4.2. Staff requirements are calculated automatically based on the venue's total capacity and event complexity.
4.3. If the regional unallocated staff count is insufficient on the event date, the creation flow will restrict organizers from choosing the "Requires Staff" option.

---

## 5. Event Cancellation & Refund Policy
5.1. Organizers may cancel their scheduled events at any time prior to the start time.
5.2. Upfront fee refunds to the organizer follow a strict schedule:
   - **Cancellation > 48 Hours:** 90% refund of the upfront fee.
   - **Cancellation 24-48 Hours:** 50% refund of the upfront fee.
   - **Cancellation < 24 Hours:** No refund of the upfront fee.
5.3. In the event of a cancellation, all purchased tickets are refunded to attendees at 100% of their face value. The Platform will handle the payouts and automatically notify all affected ticket holders via email.

---

## 6. Code of Conduct & Safety
6.1. Organizers must ensure the event content complies with local civil laws.
6.2. Any event found promoting hate speech, discrimination, illegal activities, or unsafe environments will be terminated immediately, and all ticketing proceeds will be frozen.
